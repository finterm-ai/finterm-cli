# US Equity Market Hours

## Regular Trading Hours

| Session | Eastern Time | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pre-market | 4:00 AM - 9:30 AM | Limited liquidity |
| Regular | 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM | Primary session |
| After-hours | 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM | Limited liquidity |

## Early Close Days

Markets close at 1:00 PM ET on:
- Day before Independence Day (July 3 or closest weekday)
- Day after Thanksgiving (Black Friday)
- Christmas Eve (December 24 or closest weekday)

## Time Zone Conversions

| Time Zone | Offset from ET | Market Open | Market Close |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Eastern (ET) | - | 9:30 AM | 4:00 PM |
| Central (CT) | -1h | 8:30 AM | 3:00 PM |
| Mountain (MT) | -2h | 7:30 AM | 2:00 PM |
| Pacific (PT) | -3h | 6:30 AM | 1:00 PM |
| UTC/GMT | +5h* | 2:30 PM | 9:00 PM |

*During EST; +4h during EDT (daylight saving).
